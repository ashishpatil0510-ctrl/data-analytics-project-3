Key Insights & Recommendations
🚨
Biggest Funnel Leak: Interested → Hot Lead
60.4% of interested leads never become hot leads. This is the single biggest drop-off in the funnel. Rec: Train agents to extend call quality past the 2-min mark using a structured pitch script. Goal is to push more leads past 5 minutes.
📱
Go All-In on Cellular
Cellular converts at 14.7% — 2.8× better than telephone (5.2%). Yet 36% of contacts still use telephone. Rec: Reallocate telephone budget entirely to cellular outreach. This one shift can dramatically lift overall CVR.
♻️
Re-contact Past Successes First
Previous campaign successes convert at 65.1% — the highest CVR of any segment. Rec: Always exhaust your re-contact list before reaching new leads. These leads are effectively pre-qualified and 7× easier to convert.
📅
Focus Budget on Mar, Sep, Oct, Dec
These 4 months deliver 44–51% CVR. May has 13,769 contacts but only 6.4% CVR. Rec: Shift campaign budgets toward Q4 and early Q1. Reduce May outreach by 40% and redeploy those resources to peak months.
📞
Stop After 3 Calls Per Lead
CVR drops from 13.0% at 1 call to just 3.1% at 10+ calls. Over-calling wastes agent time and budget. Rec: Implement a hard cap of 3 contact attempts per lead. Flag non-responders for re-engagement in a future campaign cycle.
🎯
Target Students, Retirees & 65+ Segments
Students convert at 31.4%, Retirees at 25.2%, and age 65+ at 46.8% — all dramatically above average. Rec: Build dedicated calling lists for these segments and train agents with segment-specific scripts for each profile.
